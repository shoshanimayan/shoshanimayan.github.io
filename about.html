About me

Hello, I'm Mayan Shoshani, a computer science student, and programmer at The University of California, Irvine.

After completing my high school Diploma at santa clara high school, I began working towards my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at UCI. Now, in the second year of my college education, I have begun to seek internships, as well as work and research opportunities to help expand my skill repertoire and increase my work experience.

My passions in both my life and my work are split between my love of technology, and my love of learning and seeing new things. I am always looking to be challanged by new problems and experiences, and I'm not afraid to learn and grow from any mistakes or hardships that may result. I would describe myself as hard working, a quick learner, and enthusiastic to cooperate with others, while also desiring to learn how to solve problems myself.

I constantly strive to belance my work effort between my instincts to be a pefectionist, and my desire to be realistic in the distribution of my effort within the restrictions of time. I think it's important to a do a job right the first time, but I also know that focusing all my effort on one part of the job means the whole will never be complete.

I am a Northern California, Bay Area native. I am a self proclaimed international traveler, and an enthusiast of film, spicy food, and modern art. I spend my free time playing video games, reading fiction, or learning new cooking recipes.
